Welcome to BearReader!

I loved the concept behind BearBlog, but I miss having a way to follow my favorite blogs and no RSS client could replicate the experience of browsing BearBlog and opening each blog entry on their own sites, so I made my own!

How does it work?

To start following blogs, just open the Manage feeds page and add the RSS links for each of them.

Then, open Timeline and the magic will happen.

Happy reading!
